Vṛṣasārasaṃgraha 13 and 14

---- kathaṃ sukhopāyo na kriyate ----

devy uvāca|
ahiṃsātithyakānāṃ ca śruto dharmaḥ suvistaraḥ|
kiṃ na kurvanti manujāḥ sukhopāyaṃ mahat phalam||13.1||
svaśarīre sthito yajñaḥ svaśarīre sthitaṃ tapaḥ|
svaśarīre sthitaṃ tīrthaṃ śruto vistarato mayā||13.2||
kimarthaṃ bhagavan brūhi sukhopāyaṃ mahat phalam|
kiṃ nivṛttās tu deveśa ṛṣidaivatamānuṣāḥ||13.3||
mahādeva uvāca|
adya pṛṣṭena kathitaṃ gopitaṃ mayi sundari|
mānuṣāṇāṃ hitārthāya tava ca varavarṇini||13.4||
adyaprabhṛti deveśi khyātir loke bhaviṣyati|
dhanyā evaṃ cariṣyanti adhanyā na ramanti tam||13.5||
triguṇena tu bandhena baddhapāśadṛḍhena tu|
tenārthena ramanty atra jānanto 'pi hi mohitāḥ||13.6||

---- triguṇabandhaḥ ----

devy uvāca|
kiṃ vā triguṇabandheti brūhi saṃśayachedaka|
adyāpi mama deveśa mohotpannas tribandhanaiḥ||13.7||
bhagavān uvāca|
prākṛtaṃ vaikṛtaṃ caiva dakṣiṇābandham eva ca|
etenaiva tu bandhena baddhāḥ varṇāśramāḥ sadā||13.8||
jñānahīnā nivartante paramaṃ prāpya tat padam|
iṣṭastrīputrabhṛtyārthe dhanadhānyasamuccaye|
snehād ākṛṣṭamanasāṃ bandhaḥ prākṛta ucyate||13.9||
yogayuktena manasā yad yad aiśvaryam āpyate|
tadā vaikṛtabandhaṃ tu yadi tatrānurajyate||13.10||
ārāmodyānavāpīṣu dānakratuphaleṣu ca|
āsaktamanaso vāso dakṣiṇābandha kathyate||13.11||
anenaiva tu pāśena baddho vānaravad yathā|
mokṣituṃ na ca śaknoti itaś cetaś ca dhāvati||13.12||
devāsuramanuṣyeṣu tiryeṣu narakeṣu ca|
bhramate cakrayantravad yāvat tattvaṃ na vindati||13.13||
garbhavāsaparikleśo janmamṛtyuḥ punaḥ punaḥ|
vyādhiśokabhayāyāsacintayā jarayā hataḥ||13.14||

---- garbhotpattiḥ ----

devy uvāca|
garbhotpattiḥ kathaṃ deva yogī labhati kīdṛśīm|
kīdṛśaṃ labhate garbhaṃ śrotuṃ naḥ pratidīyatām||13.15||
bhagavān uvāca|
śṛṇu devi pravakṣyāmi garbhotpattiṃ yathākramam|
yathā saṃśayavicchedaṃ labhiṣyasi varānane||13.16||
akṣarāt prabhavo brahmā karma brahmasamudbhavam|
karmato yajñaprabhavo yajñato dhūmasambhavaḥ||13.17||
dhūmrād abhrāṇi jāyante abhrāt parjanyasambhavaḥ |
parjanyād annam{-}utpattir annād bhūtāni jajñire||13.18||
annād rasasamutpattī rasāc choṇitasambhavaḥ|
śoṇitān māṃsam{-}utpattir māṃsād medasamudbhavaḥ||13.19||
medaso 'sthīni jāyante asthibhyo majjasambhavaḥ|
majjāyās tu bhavec chukraṃ naraḥ śukrasamudbhavaḥ||13.20||
śukraśoṇitasaṃyogād garbhotpattis tataḥ smṛtā|
agnisomātmakaṃ devi śarīraṃ dvayadhātutaḥ||13.21||
somadhātu smṛtaṃ śukram agnidhātu rajaḥ smṛtam|
agnisomāśrayaṃ devi śarīram iti saṃjñitam||13.22||
māse māse ṛtuḥ strīṇāṃ bhavatīha na saṃśayaḥ|
ṛtukāle prasarpeta na sukhārthaṃ varānane||13.23||
putrakāmaḥ prayuñjīta dharmārthaṃ ca yaśasvini|
pumān strīṣūpayuñjīta araṇīva hutāśanam||13.24||
pumān śukrādhiko jñeyaḥ kanyā raktādhikā bhavet|
samaśukre ca rakte ca sa ca jāyen napuṃsakaḥ||13.25||

---- dviyamā triyamā ca gurviṇī ----

devy uvāca|
dviyamā triyamā caiva kathaṃ jāyeta gurviṇī|
kathaṃ strīdviyamā jāyet kathaṃ vā puruṣadvayam||13.26||
bhagavān uvāca|
raktādhikā smṛtā kanyā jāyate varavarṇini|
vāyunā ca dvidhā bhinnā kanyakādviyamā smṛtā||13.27||
śukrādhikas tu puruṣo dvidhā bhinno 'nilena tu|
dviyamā puruṣā jñeyās triyamās tu tridhā kṛte||13.28||
ṛtusnātā yadā nārī yadi garbhād vigṛhṇati|
prathame ca dvitīye ca tṛtīye ca na jīvati||13.29||
sameṣu janayet putraṃ kanyakāṃ viṣame dine|
ṣaṣṭy aṣṭamī ca daśamī dvādaśī ca pumān bhavet||13.30||
pañcamī saptamī caiva navamy ekādaśī striyaḥ|
samarakte ca śukre ca śyāmaḥ saṃjāyate pumān||13.31||
rudhiraṃ tv ekarātreṇa kalalaṃ pratipadyate|
kalalaṃ pañcarātreṇa arbudatvaṃ prajāyate||13.32||
arbudaḥ saptarātreṇa māṃsapeśīsamudbhavaḥ|
dvitīyasaptarātreṇa tat sarvaṃ māṃsaśoṇitam||13.33||
tṛtīyasaptarātreṇa hṛdayaṃ jāyate tataḥ|
tataḥ sarvāṇi gātrāṇi śiraś caivopajāyate||13.34||
hṛdaye jāyamāne tu mūrcchā tandrir arocakaḥ|
striyāś chardiḥ prasekaś ca daurbalyaṃ copajāyate||13.35||
tasya hi hṛdayaṃ nārī yadi bhidyati kiṃcana|
bhakṣyaṃ lehyaṃ tathā peyam upabhogāṃs tathārthayet||13.36||
śayanāsanadānāni vastrāṇy ābharaṇāni ca|
yad yad ākāṅkṣate kiṃcit tadāsyaiva pradāpayet||13.37||
nāyāsaṃ kārayec cāsyā na caivam avamānayet|
mukham āpāṇḍuraṃ snigdhaṃ kālatvaṃ stanakakṣayoḥ||13.38||
śarīraṃ ca śriyā juṣṭaṃ pīnoruśroṇivaṅkṣaṇam|
liṅgair ebhir vijānīyād garbhe jīvaṃ pratiṣṭhitam||13.39||
caturthasaptarātreṇa śiraś caivopajāyate|
pañcamasaptarātreṇa grīvā tatropajāyate||13.40||
ṣaṣṭhamasaptarātreṇa skandhagātraṃ prajāyate|
saptamasaptarātreṇa pṛṣṭhavaṃśaḥ prajāyate||13.41||
aṣṭamasaptarātreṇa pāṇī jāyata cobhayau|
saptarātraṃ nava prāpya jāyate hy urapañjaram||13.42||
daśame saptarātre ca pādau jāyata cobhau|
udaraṃ copajāyeta saptaikādaśarātrike||13.43||
dvādaśasaptarātreṇa kukṣipārśvaḥ prajāyate|
saptatraidaśarātreṇa kaṭis tatropajāyate||13.44||
navaty aṣṭa ca rātreṇa jāyate sūtraviṃśatiḥ|
saptapañcadaśāhena sarvamedaḥ prajāyate||13.45||
ṣoḍaśasaptarātreṇa asthi sarvāṇi jāyate|
saptasaptadaśāhena jāyate snāyubandhanam||13.46||
saptamāṣṭādaśāhena jāyate mukhamaṇḍalam|
saptonaviṃśarātreṇa ghrāṇavaṃśaḥ prajāyate||13.47||
saptaviṃśatirātreṇa netranālaṃ prajāyate|
saptaikaviṃśarātreṇa karṇayugmaṃ prajāyate||13.48||
dvāviṃśasaptarātreṇa jāyate dvau bhruvau tataḥ|
saptatriviṃśarātreṇa gaṇḍayugmaṃ prajāyate||13.49||
caturviṃśatisaptāhe oṣṭhayugmaṃ prajāyate|
pañcaviṃśatisaptāhe jihvā jāyeta sundari||13.50||
ṣaḍviṃśasaptarātreṇa dantapālī prajāyate|
saptaviṃśatisaptāhe jāyate vṛṣaṇadvayam||13.51||
aṣṭāviṃśatisaptāhe bhagaliṅgaṃ prajāyate|
ūnaviṃśatisaptāhe jāyate ca tvag eva ca||13.52||
triṃśatisaptarātreṇa jāyate nābhimaṇḍalam|
saptaikatriṃśarātreṇa sarvarandhraṃ prajāyate||13.53||
dvātriṃśatsaptarātreṇa nakhaviṃśati jāyate|
tretriṃśatsaptarātreṇa roma keśaś ca jāyate||13.54||
saptarātracatustriṃśe sarvasandhiḥ prajāyate|
pañcatriṃśatisaptāhe sarvamarma prajāyate||13.55||
ṣaḍtriṃśasaptarātreṇa vedanā copajāyate|
saptatriṃśatisaptāhe īrṣādveṣaḥ prajāyate||13.56||
aṣṭatriṃśatisaptāhe pañcātmakasamanvitam|
sarvāṅgam aṅgasampūrṇaḥ paripakvaḥ sa tiṣṭhati||13.57||
mātuḥ śvāśitapītaṃ ca nābhisūtrāgamena tu|
prajātasyopadhāryante garbhasthasyaiva jantavaḥ||13.58||
tataḥ praviśate cittaṃ nidrāsvapnaṃ yathā tathā|
nopalabhyati sūkṣmatvād araṇy agnir yathā tathā||13.59||
garbhodakena siktāṅgo jarāyupariveṣṭitaḥ|
jātiṃ smarati tatrastho jantuś cetaḥsamanvitaḥ||13.60||
mṛtaś cāhaṃ punarjāto bhūyaś caiva punar mṛtaḥ|
sthāvarāṇāṃ sahasreṣu jāto 'smi vividheṣu ca||13.61||
tiryagyonisahasreṣu preteṣu narakeṣu ca|
caturvarṇavivarṇeṣu mānuṣeṣu sahasraśaḥ||13.62||
sāmprataṃ ca punar garbhaḥ kleśaprāptaḥ suduḥsahaḥ|
idānīṃ jātamātro 'haṃ saṃskāraiś cāpi saṃskṛtaḥ||13.63||
yogam evābhisevāmi sāṃkhyaṃ vā pañcaviṃśakam|
yatra janmajarā nāsti yatra mṛtyuś ca nāsti vai||13.64||
yatra brahma paraṃ vaidyaṃ cariṣyāmi yatavrataḥ|
evam ādīny anekāni cintayitvā punaḥ punaḥ||13.65||
yāvat tiṣṭhati garbhastho jātiṃ smarati pūrvikām|
tato jāyati kaṣṭena mahākleśena mānavaḥ||13.66||
yoniyantrasutīvreṇa pīḍyamānaḥ suduḥkhitaḥ|
jātamātre smṛtibhraṃśo bhavatīha acetanaḥ||13.67||
māyāmudgaratīvreṇa hataḥ kiṃ śubham ācaret|
eṣa garbhasamutpattiḥ kathito 'smi varānane|
duḥkhasaṃsāraprathamaḥ kiṃ bhūyaḥ śrotum icchasi||13.68||

||iti vṛṣasārasaṃgrahe garbhotpattir adhyāyas trayadaśamaḥ||





---- deharūpavarṇabhedāni ----

devy uvāca|
atidīrgho 'tihrasvaś ca pumān kenopajāyate|
atigauro 'tikṛṣṇaś ca naro bhavati kiṃ prabho||14.1||
bhagavān uvāca|
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī nityam uttānaśālinī|
prasāritavibhaktātmā so 'tidīrghaḥ prajāyate||14.2||
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī śete saṃkucitā sadā|
rasānnādīni kaṭukaṃ sevanā hrasva jāyate||14.3||
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī nityaṃ kṣīropasevinī|
varakodravaśālīṃś ca bhuṅkte cāpi yavaudanam|
śuklavastrasrajā yuktā sātigauraṃ prasūyate||14.4||
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī kāladhānyāni sevate|
māṣakṛṣṇatilāmudgaṃ tathā kṛṣṇayavodanam|
kṛṣṇavastrasrajādīni tasyāḥ kṛṣṇaḥ prajāyate||14.5||

---- jātidoṣāni ----

devy uvāca|
jātyandho jāyate kasmāt ṣaṇḍho bhīrur hatendriyaḥ|
kubjo vā vāmano vāpi paṇḍaḥ sthūlaśiraḥ katham||14.6||
bhagavān uvāca|
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī tīkṣṇoṣṇāny upasevate|
laśunāni palāṇḍūni karañjamūlakāni ca||14.7||
pippalīṃ śṛṅgaveraṃ ca sarṣapān maricāni ca|
āsavaṃ ca parikliṣṭā ye cānye kaṭutiktakāḥ|
tīkṣṇaṃ tu sevamānā yā jātyandhaṃ janayet sutam||14.8||
mithyopacārāḥ strīpuṃso vyāpanne śukraśoṇite|
yadā garbhāśaye raktaṃ striyāḥ pūrvaṃ niṣicyate|
paścāc chukraṃ raktakāle tadā ṣaṇḍaḥ prasūyate||14.9||
trastodvignā yadā bhītā strī puṃsā sūyate prajā|
tatra yo jāyate garbhād bhīruḥ krandanako bhavet||14.10||
visargakāle śukrasya vighna utpadyate yadā|
indriyāvartavighne tu tadā jāyed anindriyaḥ||14.11||
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī vātalāny upasevate|
kaṭukāni kaṣāyāni tiktāni ca viśeṣataḥ||14.12||
vātaḥ prakupitas tasyā garbham ābhujya tiṣṭhati|
kubjas tu jāyate tasmād garbhād vātanipīḍanāt||14.13||
nityam āsanaśīlā yā tathā cotkuṭakāsanā|
tasyāḥ saṃhanyate garbho vāmanas tena jāyate||14.14||
ativyāyāmaśīlā tu yā nārī viṣamāsanī|
garbhaḥ saṃkṣubhyate tasyāḥ paṇḍas tenopajāyate||14.15||
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī rūḍhadhānyāni sevate|
vātaśleṣma śirastho vai tasya garbhasya kupyate|
tataḥ sthūlaśirās tena pumān jāyaty asaṃśayaḥ||14.16||
devy uvāca|
karālāṅgā hanuḥ paṅgur mūko gadgadabhāṣakaḥ|
vivṛtākṣas tv anakṣo vā bhaved duḥkhagudaḥ katham||14.17||
bhagavān uvāca|
karālastanadoṣeṇa jāyate mānavas tathā|
atha karālaṃ kurute nārī lamboticūcukā|
tasmād etena doṣeṇa karālo jāyate pumān||14.18||
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī raktapittāmayārditā|
gohanuṃ janayaty eṣā raktapittaprakopitā||14.19||
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī vātaśūlair upadrutā|
śukrodāvartanī cāpi paṅguṃ janayate sutam||14.20||
kṣudhārtā vedanārtā ca satataṃ copavāsinī|
mūkaṃ janayate putraṃ dauhṛdaṃ ca vimānitā||14.21||
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī visṛjen māsamāsikam|
anakṣo jāyate tasyā garbhaśoṇitasaṃkṣayāt||14.22||
arthagrastā yadā nārī vātodāvartapīḍitā|
gṛhītagarbhā rūkṣāṇi vātalāny upasevate||14.23||
vātasthānantatas tasyā garbhasyāpīḍitaṃ bhavet|
agudo jāyate tasmāj jātaś cāpi na jīvati||14.24||
devy uvāca|
hīnāṅgo jāyate kasmād adhikāṅgo 'pi vā katham|
śvetapiṅgekṣaṇaḥ kasmāt kathaṃ lohitalocanaḥ||14.25||
bhagavān uvāca|
garbhasya jāyamānasya - - -  jāyate nilaḥ|
vātābhyāṃ śleṣmaṇāt - - - tadaṅgaṃ parihīyate||14.26||
hīnāṅgo jāyate tasmāt pumān vātaprakopataḥ|
gṛhītagarbhā yā nārī madhurāṇy upasevate||14.27||
śṛṅgāṭakakalotyāni śālūkāni viśāni ca|
mocaṃ tālaphalaṃ caiva nārikelaphalaṃ tathā||14.28||
atikṣṇaṃ sevamānā tu adhikāṅgaṃprasūyate|
piṅgākṣaḥ śleṣmapittābhyāṃ śvetākṣaḥ śleṣmaṇā bhavet||14.29||
devy uvāca|
kathaṃ vā jāyate putraḥ kanyakā kena jāyate|
apumān kena jāyeta dviyamā triyamā tathā||14.30||
bhagavān uvāca|
śukrādhikaḥ pumān jñeyaḥ kanyā raktādhikā bhavet|
raktaśukrasamatvena jāyate sa napuṃsakaḥ||14.31||
piṇḍībhūto yadā garbha mārutau vibhaved dvidhā|
evaṃ te dviyamā jñeyās triyamā ca tridhā kṛte||14.32||
devy uvāca|
śoṇitaṃ māṃsa medaś ca asthi majjā ca pañcamī|
śarīrasthāni dṛśyante śukrasthānaṃ na dṛśyate||14.33||
tasyotpattiś ca sthānaṃ ca jñātum icchāmi tattvataḥ|
kathayasva trilokeśa cchettum arhasi saṃśayaḥ||14.34||
bhagavān uvāca|
manaḥ śukrasya prabhavaṃ ghrāṇaṃ śrotraṃ tathākṣiṇī|
sthānaṃ tu sarvāṅgasamasparśāt sparśaḥ pravartate||14.35||
yathā niṣiktaṃ kṣīraṃ tu payasād dadhi jāyate|
pramathyamānadadhnas tu sarpiso 'pi tathāgamaḥ||14.36||
evaṃ śarīra nirgaccet - śukraṃ śukravahā śirāḥ|
pūrayitvānupūrveṇa asthayo pratipadyate||14.37||
tatas tu tāḥ śukravahā meḍhranābhīm anusṛtāḥ|
nāśukraṃ tat tu siñcanti tasmād garbhasya sambhavaḥ||14.38||
devy uvāca|
kathaṃ vedayate jāti kathaṃ jātismaro bhavet|
etasmin saṃśayaṃ me 'dya chettum arhasi śaṅkara||14.39||
bhagavān uvāca|
bhāvitātmāṃ ca yo jantur devi bhogādhikaṃ ca yat|
brahmavid jñānasaṃyuktaḥ sa jātiṃ smarate pumān||14.40||
devy uvāca|
kathaṃ sadyo gṛhītasya liṅgagarbhasya dṛśyate|
etat kathaya deveśa rahaḥ kāle maheśvara||14.41||
bhagavān uvāca|
pipāśāromaharṣaṃ ca vepanaṃ gātrasīdanam|
nidrāsvedaṃ ca tandrā ca muhūrtam upajāyate||14.42||
nikledatvaṃ kharatvaṃ ca yonyāt samupajāyate|
na cārdravaṃvai dṛśyeta śukrasya rajaso 'pi vā|
sadyogṛhītagarbhāyā liṅgāny etāni tattvataḥ||14.43||
devy uvāca|
kena liṅgena vijñeyaṃ putrajanma maheśvara|
kanyakā kena liṅgena jñāyate kathayasva me||14.44||
bhagavān uvāca|
pādorujaṅghapārśvaś ca dakṣiṇaṃ yadi hy unnataḥ|
dakṣiṇaṃ vipulaṃ tatra tadā putraḥ prajāyate||14.45||
vāmaś caiva yadā paśyet tadā jāyeta kanyakā|
unnataṃ madhyamasthāś ca tadā jāyet - napuṃsakam||14.46||
devy uvāca|
puṃsā kapolaromāni khalitaṃ kena jāyate|
kathaṃ strīṇāṃ na jāyeta romāṇi khalitaṃ tathā||14.47||
bhagavān uvāca|
tathā vṛṣaṇagā jantor yasya retovahā śiraḥ|
nibaddhā mastake tālu kapolās tu samāśritāḥ||14.48||
taiḥ kapoleṣu romāṇi jāyante antaretasaḥ|
khalitaṃ śukradoṣeṇa narāṇām upajāyate||14.49||
śirā śukravahā strīṇāṃ na śūnyasyānna jāyate|
yātmāpālo ca kās tv agni dṛṣṭimaṇḍalasaṃśritaḥ ?||14.50||
śoṇitai soktikoṣṭasthanniśoṣayati tattvataḥ|
nibaddhanty akṣipakṣmāṇi tena romāṇi ca bhruvoḥ||14.51||
aśukratvāc ca nārīṇāṃ khalitaṃ nopajāyate|
chāyāvyapagatasnehā rukṣāgātraśiroruhā|
grasatosmābhajaṭharā mṛtagarbhaḥ prajāyate||14.52||
devy uvāca|
somadhātu kathaṃ jñeyā agnidhātus tatheśvara|
pṛthagbhāgaviśeṣeṇa kathayasva maheśvara||14.53||
bhagavān uvāca|
śleṣmamedas tathā snāyuḥ asthidantanakhāni ca|
striyās tanyaś ca śukraś ca yac ca śvetaṃ tathākṣiṣu||14.54||
eteṣāṃ saumyabhāgatvāc chvetatvam upajāyate|
āgneyabhāvād raktatvaṃ kṛṣṇatvaṃ cāpi gacchati||14.55||
tvagmāṃsarudhiraṃ majjādṛṣṭiroma tathaiva ca|
āgneyadhātu somaś ca kathito 'smi varānane|
brūhi brūhi viśālākṣi yady asti tava saṃśayaḥ||14.56||

||iti vṛṣasārasaṃgrahe praśnavyākaraṇo nāmaś caturdaśo 'dhyāyaḥ||
