11:35:19 From Yuko Yokochi To Everyone:
	\textbf{pūtikarṇaḥ} ¦ puṃ, (pūtirdurgandhaḥ karṇo yasmāt |)
	karṇarogaviśeṣaḥ |
	tallakṣaṇaṃ yathā— 
	pūtikarṇam āha
	“karṇavidradhipākādvā ma karṇo vāripūraṇāt |
	pūyaṃ sravati yat pūti sa jñeyaḥ pūtikarṇakaḥ ||”
	karṇasrāvādbhedārthamāha |
	pūtīti niyamena pūti yathā syādevaṃ sravati ||
	taccikitsā yathā— 
	“karṇasrave pūtikarṇe tathaiva kṛmikarṇake |
	 sāmānyaṃ karma kurvīta yogān vaiśeṣikānapi ||
	sarjjikācūrṇasaṃyuktabījapūrarasaṃ kṣipet |
	karṇasrāvarujā dāhāstena naśyantyasaṃśayam 1 ||
	āmrajambupravālānāṃ madhukasya vaṭasya ca |
	ebhistu sādhitaṃ tailaṃ pūtikarṇagadaṃ haret 2 ||
	jātīpatrarase tailaṃ vipakvaṃ pūtikarṇajit 3 ||
	ghṛṣṭaṃ rasāñjanaṃ nāryāḥ kṣīreṇa kṣaudrasaṃyutam |
	praśasyate cirotthe tat sāsrāve pūtikarṇake 4 ||
	kuṣṭhahiṅguvacādāruśatāhvāviśvasaindhavaiḥ |
	pūtikarṇāpahaṃ tailaṃ vastamūtreṇa sādhitam ||
